NAUKASANA

  • Lie flat on the floor with hands on the sides. Let the palms face downwards.
  • Start Inhaling and simultaneously raise the legs, arms and the upper body.
  • The weight of the body will rest entirely on the buttocks. In the raised position,
    the toes should line up with the palms.
  • Hold the breath and remain in this position for as long as you are comfortable.
  • While returning to the original position exhale.
  • Relax the entire body and remain in Shavasana until you are ready to repeat the process.

BENEFITS

  • Naukasana strengthens the abdominal muscles.
  • It helps to remove belly fat.
  • It improves digestion.
  • Naukasana tones and improves the health of all organs in the abdomen especially the liver, pancreas and kidneys.
  • It strengthens the muscles of the arms, thighs and shoulders.
  • It stimulates intestinal peristalsis.
