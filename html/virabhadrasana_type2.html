FOR BEGINNERS

VIRABHADRASANA TYPE 2

  • Assume the Virabhadrasana – I position. After releasing the pose, come back to the standing pose.
  • Turn the right foot 90 degrees to the front. The left foot is facing sideways as in Virabhadrasana – I.
  • Stretch both hands and place then parallel to the ground, aligning them along the shoulders.
  • The head, chest, left knee and foot should face the left direction.
    Gaze forward along the direction of the left foot.
  • Take slow deep breaths and remain in this position as long as you are comfortable.
  • After releasing the pose, the same can be tried on the right side.

BENEFITS

  • Virabhadrasana gives a good stretch to the leg muscles.
  • It is good for the organs in the abdomen.
  • It can improve flexibility of the back muscles.
  • It improves the nervous balance of the body.
  • It can relieve stress in the shoulders.
