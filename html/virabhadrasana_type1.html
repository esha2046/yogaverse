FOR BEGINNERS

VIRABHADRASANA TYPE 1

  • Stand straight with the legs spread out. Let there be distance of at least 3 feet between the two feet.
  • Raise the hands above the head and the palms facing each other.
  • Turn to the left and slowly rotate the left foot about 90 degrees to the left so that it is aligned with the chest.
  • Turn the right foot slightly towards the inside.
  • Bend the left knee till the thighs are parallel to the ground. The right leg should remain straight.
  • The head, the chest, the left knee and the left foot should be aligned in one direction and bring the palms
    closer so that they touch each other.
  • Retain this position for few breaths, which should be slow and deep.

BENEFITS

  • Virabhadrasana gives a good stretch to the leg muscles.
  • It is good for the organs in the abdomen.
  • It can improve flexibility of the back muscles.
  • It improves the nervous balance of the body.
  • It can relieve stress in the shoulders.
