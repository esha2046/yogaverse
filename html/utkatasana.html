FOR INTERMEDIATE

UTKATASANA

  • Stand erect with both the feet spaced apart.
  • Take your hands forwards with palms facing downwards, till it is horizontal to floor.
  • Now imagine that you are going to sit on an imaginary chair. Bend your knees and try to sit down on the
    imaginary chair.
  • Once you have reached this position, the thighs will make a 45 degrees angle with the lower legs.
  • Raise the hands up and bring them alongside your ears.
  • Try to breathe normally in the pose and relax your mind.
  • You may try to go up and down the seat, as if trying to slightly get up with from the chair and then sitting in it
    again. This will strengthen your leg and thigh muscles.

BENEFITS

  • Utkatasana strengthens the hips and lower back muscles.
  • It gives balance and stability to the body.
  • It strengthens the arms, legs and thigh muscles.
  • It tones the knees and ankles.
